## States With Zero Coverage Containing Top-50 Metros

**NY, TN, MN, NV, OK, UT, RI** — these 7 states have zero rate data and contain 9 of the top 50 metros. They represent the single biggest opportunity for coverage improvement.

---

## Caveats

- **Name-matching heuristic**, not true MSA boundary analysis. Suburban utilities in a metro won't match.
- Pop figures are SDWIS `population_served` for matched utilities, not Census MSA population — undercounts total metro population.
- Some metros (Atlanta, Orlando) may have coverage from state bulk ingests that didn't match the city name search.
- Coverage percentages reflect the primary utility systems only. Many metros have dozens of smaller systems not captured here.
